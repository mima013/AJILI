About Us

Ajili Mine Rdc

We are a Congolese company with majority Congolese capital, founded in the year 2022 with the primary goal to provide services in supply chain and civil engineering. With a growing demand for engineering services in the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC).


Who we are

We are focused on our ability to manufacture products and provide services at competitive rates. AJILI MINE RDC has committed:

  • To assure that the quality policy is the main concern of all company staff.
  • To attest high quality services to our existing and potential clients
  • To promote a culture of professionalism and respect for all cultures
  • To have the ability to complete and deliver all scale projects.

Vision

  • Providing services to our clients with a highly professional and project management team.
  • To establish trusting relationship with all our stakeholders
  • To give the best to our agents so they give the best of themselves in their families, in their lives and in company.

Mission

  • To be the leader and a flexible, adaptable organization with international standards in the African market.
